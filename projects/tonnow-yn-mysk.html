Tonnow Yn Mysk

Last Updated: 23 Jan 2021

Information:

Written in C# using the Unity Game Engine. Tonnow Yn Mysk was created as part of the GAM110 module at Falmouth University. I worked in a team with 8 other students across various games disciplines. Consisting of 1 Artist, 1 Animator, 3 Designers, 4 Programmers and 1 Writer. Whilst audio isn't my speciality, in the team, I was the most experienced when it came to Foley. This lead to me doing both programming and foley.

Game Details:

Premise

You live as a humble fisherman. Your job, simple, catch as many fish as you can and avoid the monster!

Jam Theme

The game was created as part of the Creative Card Jam at Falmouth Univeristy over a 4 week period.
The theme for the Game Jam was Cornish Mythology with a unique team modifier of Escape.

Controls

Left Arrow - Sail Left
Right Arrow - Sail Right
Left Shift - Cast Reel
