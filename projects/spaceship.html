Spaceship

Last Updated: 25 Jan 2021

Information:

Written in C# using the Unity Game Engine. Created for the Weekly Game Jam 118 which had the theme of "Spaceship". Delve into this simplistic, 8-bit spaceship game; Fight hordes of enemy ships and upgrade your ship until you're an unstoppable force. The game, simply titled "Spaceship" pits you against enemies in the depths of space. How far will you get?

Game Details:

Controls:

A/Left Arrow - Move Left
D/Right Arrow - Move Right
Space - Shoot
Escape - Close
